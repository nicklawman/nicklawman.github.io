The heart and first step in creating your song is the lyrics. Without them, it’s just a random, soulless piece generated by AI—devoid of meaning for you. In the past, crafting lyrics was challenging, but today, you can collaborate with ChatGPT to create lyrics that truly belong to you and speak for you.

The key is to express your ideas, thoughts, experiences, or even a story you’ve written. For example, in my second album, the track “Eye Drops, Words Unsaid” was based on a story I wrote. I worked closely with ChatGPT, discussing and refining the lyrics until they perfectly conveyed the essence of my story.

Remember, ChatGPT is a magnifier: if you have nothing to input, it will only produce trivial output. But if you have something meaningful to express, ChatGPT will help you find the right words. However, it doesn’t know you personally, so be patient and engage in multiple rounds of conversation until you’re satisfied with the result.

The next step is to use an AI music generator to create music that complements your lyrics. This process requires a keen sense of musical taste. Tools like Suno (https://suno.com) or SongGenerator (https://songgenerator.io) make it easy and accessible, offering free daily quotas to help you produce a song that sings your lyrics. However, not every output will be a masterpiece.

It’s up to you, like a director, to choose the best band and singer to fully express your lyrics and bring your vision to life. In the end, it’s your soul infused into the lyrics and your taste that makes the AI-generated song unique. This journey is extraordinary—what once was a daunting task is now something you can do yourself, creating a song for yourself, by yourself. Enjoy the process.
